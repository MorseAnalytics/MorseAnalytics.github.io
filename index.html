Welcome to Morse Analytics

Through Morse Analytics, I help people to make sense of data. My approach is both advisory and technical: gaining insight from complex data sets that can support better decision-making; developing effective ways to communicate information; and setting up systems for collecting and managing information. I analyse data, design data structures, set up databases, create spreadsheets, and develop bespoke apps.

Gaining insight

Simply having access to a comprehensive data set is not enough to help your organisation in its decisions, unless you have a good grasp of what it tells you and how you can use it for the organisation’s benefit. I have extensive experience in helping organisations construct an approach in understanding the meaning of their data, and applying it to support decision-making.

Effective communication

Simply creating tables, charts and dashboards of your data may not be enough to articulate what you really need to say. Do you need to build a model using the data to simulate an environment? I can help to design and construct models, and and how those models can be effectively communicated and utilised through a range of media, from Excel-based approaches to web-based ones.

Developing systems

You may want to collect essential data to support your organisation, but don’t have the tools to do so. I have extenseive experience in designing and building bespoke tools to help organisations collect and manage essential data effectively.

Whilst most of my experience has been gained with healthcare-related organisations, I have also worked with the sport & fitness, meeting management, training and careers industries. To provide examples of some of the approaches I have taken, I have developed a number of real-life case studies, which can be found here.

I have a very flexible approach to a working relationship. I have consulted with most of the major pharmaceutical companies and the National Health Service, set up analytical services departments, provided training and mentoring to individuals, partnered with other freelancers, and subcontracted for corporations.
